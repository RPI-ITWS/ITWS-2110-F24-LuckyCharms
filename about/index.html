About LIMBS

Meet the Project and the Team Behind It

Like how our title suggests, we are a essentially a lab inventory tracker that helps professors and other shop owners keep track of who is checking out or using the lab equipment at all times. This way, professors like Dr. Callahan might not lose an important piece of lab equipment again (or at least will be better equipped if it does happen)!

The Team consists of six member of Dr. Callahan's Web Systems class that sought out to make his lab life better:
